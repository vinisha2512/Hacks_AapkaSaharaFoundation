AAPKA SAHARA
DASHBOARD

Login

1. Go to Home page.
2. Click on LOGIN button.
3. If you have your account created then enter email and password to proceed further.
4. Press login button.
5. Congratulations you have signed into your account.

Sign Up

1. Go to Home page.
2. Click on SIGNUP button.
3. Enter the details required to fill the form.
4. Press Sign in button.
5. Congratulations you have successfully created your account.

Aadhaar Verification

1. Click on Choose file button.
2. Select image of your Aadhaar QR code.
3. Press upload to verify.

Schedule Meeting

1. Choose the volunteer nearby your location.
2. Select date and time for the meeting.
3. Enter your phone number.
4. Press SCHEDULE to create a meeting.
5. To check whether the meeting is scheduled, go back and press CHECK REPORT on EVENTS.
